Our Solutions

We provide comprehensive and
affordable air quality monitoring
solutions for both indoor and outdoor
enviornment.
Offices

Poor indoor air quality affects health, productivity,
decision making, and absenteeism in the
workspace.

Schools

Children spend more than 1500 hours each year
in school. Air pollution exposure can cause
irreversible damage to their lungs which are still
developing.

Service Industry

Premium hotels, malls, gyms, salons, restaurants
etc. have an opportunity to differentiate
themselves to their clients by managing and
showcasing good air quality on their premises.

City Networks

Regulatory grade sensors are extremely
expensive to purchase, deploy, and maintain.
Low-cost sensors can allow cities to create
dense air quality networks to understand local
hotspots and take focused action.

Grassroots & Institutes

Various organizations are working tirelessly to
drive awareness on the ground and require
monitoring to help educate communities on the
harmful effects of air pollution.Top institutes
across the country are working on research
which requires monitoring equipment to move
our understanding of air quality forward.

Construction Sites

Various regulations now require construction
sites to monitor air quality continuously and
share data with regulatory bodies.
